Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix Gulab Jamun powder with water to form a smooth dough, then shape into small balls.
  2. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom until slightly thickened.
  3. Deep fry the dough balls in ghee or oil until golden brown.
  4. Soak the fried balls in the warm sugar syrup for a few hours before serving.
